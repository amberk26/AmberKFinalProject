Pistachio Croissant

A bakery's fresh pistachio croissant is a delightful fusion of flaky, buttery pastry and the rich, nutty flavor of pistachios. The croissant is perfectly golden-brown, with layers of delicate, crisp pastry that melt in your mouth with each bite. Inside, it’s filled with a smooth, sweet pistachio cream that adds a luxurious richness, complementing the nutty crunch of pistachios scattered throughout. The combination of textures—the crisp, airy layers and the creamy, nutty center—creates a balanced, indulgent experience. Often topped with chopped pistachios and a light glaze, this croissant is a delicious treat that pairs perfectly with a warm cup of coffee or tea.
