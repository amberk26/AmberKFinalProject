Biscotti

Fresh biscotti from Jackson's Bakery are crisp, twice-baked Italian cookies that offer the perfect balance of crunch and flavor. Made with care in small batches, these golden, oblong treats are baked to a firm, satisfying texture that holds up beautifully when dipped in coffee or tea. They come in a variety of flavors—such as classic almond, chocolate-dipped, pistachio-cranberry, or hazelnut—with each bite delivering a toasted, nutty richness and subtle sweetness. Unlike mass-produced versions, fresh bakery biscotti are often slightly more tender in the center, with a rustic, handmade charm that elevates them from simple cookie to refined indulgence.
