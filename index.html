Chocolate Pie

This chocolate cream pie is a rich, dessert featuring a buttery, flaky pie crust filled with a smooth, velvety chocolate custard or pudding. The filling is typically made from a mixture of milk or cream, sugar, egg yolks, and melted chocolate, thickened with cornstarch or flour for a creamy, silky texture.

Pistachio Croissant

pistachio croissant is a luxurious twist on the classic French pastry, combining the flaky layers of a traditional croissant with the rich, nutty flavor of pistachios. It's filled with a sweet pistachio cream from ground pistachios, butter, sugar, and eggs, the croissant is baked a second time to deepen its flavor and texture.

Chocolate Donuts

These Chocolate donuts with cream filling are indulgent pastries that combine a rich, cocoa-infused dough with a luscious, sweet cream center. These donuts are deep-fried to achieve a tender, fluffy interior and a slightly crisp outer shell, then coated in a glossy chocolate glaze.
