Coffee Cake

The coffee cake at Jackson's Bakery is a warm, comforting treat known for its moist, tender crumb and signature cinnamon-sugar swirl. Baked in generous slices or square portions, this cake is rich yet light, it is featured with a buttery, vanilla-scented base. A crunchy streusel topping made with brown sugar, cinnamon, and sometimes chopped nuts adds texture and a sweet, spiced finish. Served slightly warm or at room temperature, it pairs perfectly with a cup of coffee or tea, making it a favorite morning indulgence or afternoon pick-me-up.
